About

The story of Morshu's Market and where we are heading from here

Morshu's Market is an online shopping experience for anyone who wants new or old games. Our Stock is ever expanding and we should be selling around 50 seperate games by the end of this year(Thats more than 5x the amount we offer now!!!).

This website started in 2021 when I had the idea to do a final project for school on an online game store. The logo was made after an image me and my brother found one day. The image was of the Titular Morshu with a thumbs up, around him was a red circle with yellow writting spelling 'Morshu Approved' [LINK]. We would joke about it from time to time and when I told him about the website I was making he said I should sneak the image in somewhere. Thats when I thought, 'hey, why don't I just make it my logo' a few modifications later Morshu's Market was born.

The main game, Giuseppe Kombat, is also a joke me and my brother had that hopefully someday might become a reality. It was based off a bootleg game in a Giuseppe pizza ad that looked like a cross between 'Mortal Kombat' and 'Ninja Gaiden'. This is where the name came from and why Ryu from 'Ninja Gaiden' is in it.

Althoght this website isn't actually real, it is something that in the future might be, as it would be great to open an online gaming store, with expanded stock of course.

That being said if there is any game that you feel would be good on a website like this or have any general feedback on the website, send an email through the contacts tab at the top of the page (and yes, it is a real email although it is not checked often).
